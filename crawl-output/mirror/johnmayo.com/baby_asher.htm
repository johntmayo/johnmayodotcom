A lot of people on my e mail list have asked about the baby they prayed so earnestly for back in April and May.  Asher is doing well.  Doctors had told the family when born he would live at the most just minutes and he should be with his parents in his few minutes of life.  A priest was in the room to administer Last Rites.

The baby is doing well, will have some rehabilitation treatment at Shriners in Shreveport, no surgery as of now, and appears to be on the road for a near normal physical life.  Here is his picture.
