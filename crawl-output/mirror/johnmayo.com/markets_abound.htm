No, no, no Wal Marts.  Plenty of open air or covered markets.  They are eclectic, fun, and smell just as I remembered from 40 years ago.  They are everywhere.  There is one or two upscale western style department stores.  And in Can Tho there was a store very much like the Ben Franklin Five and Dime or an early Woolworth.  I really did like the color at the markets.  Take your chance, though, if you are a westerner on eating the food.  You'll see what I mean.
