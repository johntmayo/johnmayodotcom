From John Mayo

Personal Notes

When I started this site I had two sections. As you can see I have added several more. "I Was a Soldier Once..." is a series of pages of pictures of Vietnam and what I recall I was doing when they were taken. Others include thoughts are what we are doing while in session and some other comments.

On July 8, 2008, I added a new section following my return from a visit to Vietnam. I would hope you take the time to view these pages. I would hope if you know a Vietnam Vet, you let him or her know that we did, indeed, win the "Hearts and Minds" of the people. "Return to Vietnam".

If you wish to contact me regarding any issue, please email to either john@johnmayo.com (my home) or jmayo@mail.house.state.ms.us while we are in session (Jan-April).

If you want to be added to receive "LEGISLATIVE UPDATES", e mail me at jmayo@mail.house.state.ms.us.

By the way, this is my personal site and not a government sponsored, paid, or managed site. My cell phone number is 662 902 8633.
